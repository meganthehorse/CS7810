Shared properties (Nasa-JPL and MP3C):

number - asteroid identification number
e - Orbital eccentricity
a - semi-major axis
q - perihelion distance
i - inclination; angle with respect to x-y ecliptic plane
node - longitude of the ascending node
peri (nasa jpl), ω (mp3c) - argument of perihelion
M (nasa jpl) - Mean anomaly 
m (mp3c) - Mean anomaly at the epoch
tp - time of perihelion passage
period - sidereal orbital period
n - mean daily motion
Q - aphelion distance


MP3C Specific: 

Epoch
n obs - Number of observations (MPC)
n opp - Number of oppositions (MPC)
Year First Observed
Year Last Observed
rms - RMS residual
G - Slope parameter
H- Absolute magnitude
Mass
D - Diameter
1/D - Inverse Diameter
(1/D) err - Inverse Diameter Error
pV - Geometric visible albedo

Asterrank Specific: 

Type, Group - Asteroid classifications
Value - Asterrank's estimated value
Est. Profit - Asterrank's estimated profit
Δv - Average velocity
MOID - Minimum orbit intersection distance
